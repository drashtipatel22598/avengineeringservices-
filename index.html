Who We Are

Established in 2021, AV Engineering Services specializes in supplying spares for power plants and the oil and gas industry. We excel in overhauling and repairing turbines and generators with capacities up to 1000 MW.

Our team also provides expert technical field advisors for renowned brands such as MHI, GE, ALSANDO, and Siemens. Known for our robust, efficient, and prompt service, AV Engineering Services has built a strong reputation in the industry for delivering exceptional quality and reliability.
